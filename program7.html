About the College

Don Bosco College, Mannuthy, a Catholic Minority Institution, is a part of the aforesaid worldwide network. As such, it aims at enlightening the lives of students, staff and parents with the values of the Gospel and to impart to the students a holistic education. It is an Arts and Science college affiliated to university of Calicut, where we strive to achieve the total development of youngsters and transform them into honest citizens and persons of faith. It provides university education needed to meet the challenges of time. The Government of Kerala granted permission to start the college through the GO (MS) No. 10/2005/Higher Edn. Thiruvananthapuram, dated 14/02/2005.
